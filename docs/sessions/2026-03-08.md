# 2026-03-08 Session: Initial Build

## What was built
- **Dashboard (index.html)**: Redaction reveal hero, situation report with 6 key stats, contested information (Iranian casualties, US casualties, Landstuhl), media blackout section (credentials, Stars and Stripes, Minab silence block), simultaneous operations list, action items
- **Timeline (timeline.html)**: Full chronology 2018-present with scope note linking to CFR backgrounder. 25+ sourced events covering JCPOA withdrawal, Israel-Iran escalation, Twelve-Day War, Iranian massacres, failed negotiations, Operation Epic Fury launch, Rubio admission, shifting rationales, Landstuhl, War Powers votes, NATO/Turkey missile
- **Impact (impact.html)**: Strait of Hormuz, energy prices, cost to taxpayers, markets, supply chain, water/desalination, environmental damage, cultural heritage, cybersecurity, shipping insurance, civilian infrastructure, geopolitical positions, humanitarian crisis, public opinion
- **Data system**: data.json centralizes all changing stats. app.js loads via data-bind attributes. HTML retains hardcoded fallbacks.
- **Project structure**: CLAUDE.md, session logs, GitHub Actions link checker

## Research conducted
- 8 parallel research agents covering: current conflict status, media censorship, international sources/casualties, prelude/Israel role, economic impact, environmental/cultural/water damage, German hospital (Landstuhl) lead, domain availability
- All statistics traced to named sources with URLs

## Editorial decisions
- No editorializing or drawing conclusions for readers
- Disputed figures shown side by side from all sources
- Landstuhl section presents verified facts and context without implying a conclusion
- Defense contractor stock gains left out (implies "who profits")
- Protests left out (covering one side is bias, both sides adds complexity without clarity)
- Veterans/VA benefits left out (juxtaposition is editorializing)
- No red in design (too on-the-nose for war). Black, white, gray only.
- No em dashes
